Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and services

Why are your prices higher than accessibility widgets?

▼

Accessibility widgets are overlay solutions that don't actually fix the underlying code — they provide zero legal protection and have been actively rejected in court. We fix every violation at the code level, provide screen reader testing, and back it with a legally defensible compliance certificate. Our pricing reflects real work that actually protects you.

What does "starting at" mean for pricing?

▼

Our pricing depends on the number of violations we find and the size of your website. The "starting at" price is the minimum for a simple site with few violations. We provide a fixed-price quote within 24 hours of your free scan — you'll know the exact cost before any work begins.

Do you charge ongoing fees?

▼

No. All our pricing is one-time, fixed fee. Once we've fixed your site and provided your compliance certificate, you're done. No subscriptions, no monthly payments, no hidden costs.

How long does the work take?

▼

Quick Scan: 24 hours. Full Fix: 3–7 days depending on site size and violations. Full Compliance Bundle: 5–10 days. We use AI-assisted development tools to work faster than traditional agencies without sacrificing quality.

What if new violations appear after you fix my site?

▼

If you add new content or make changes that introduce new violations, we offer discounted follow-up audits to catch and fix them. Your compliance certificate covers the state of your site at the time of delivery — we recommend periodic audits if you frequently update content.

Is the compliance certificate legally defensible?

▼

Yes. Our certificate includes before/after documentation, verification scan results, and detailed remediation records. This is the kind of documentation courts and plaintiff attorneys actually respect. Widget certificates have been rejected — our approach is based on actual code fixes.
