The Problem: Your Website is Probably Breaking the Law

Accessibility lawsuits have become a cottage industry. In 2025, over 5,100 ADA cases were filed — that's 14 every working day.

⚖️ Legal Reality

  • ADA Title III (US): Any business with a website can be sued by anyone claiming they cannot access it.
  • Equality Act 2010 (UK): Disabled users have a legal right to equal access. Non-compliance = discrimination.
  • European Accessibility Act: €3 million fines if you sell to Europe and aren't compliant.
  • 95.9% of top 1M websites have violations. Yours almost certainly does too.
